Willian Caprino is one of the researchers behind the documentary on the Brazilian hacker scene — a project dedicated to recovering stories, key figures, and defining moments of hacking in the country. Together with Carlos Cabral, he investigates and documents accounts of influential people who helped shape Brazil’s hacker community.

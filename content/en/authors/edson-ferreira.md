Edson Ferreira is a cybersecurity analyst focused on Wi-Fi security, pentesting, and digital privacy. He holds an MBA in Cybersecurity, a Google Cybersecurity certification, and a degree in Systems Analysis and Development. He works on raising awareness about real-world risks in mobile environments and wireless networks.

Giuliano Sanfins (0x_alibabas) is a Red Team analyst at SiDi, focused on Windows vulnerability research and offensive security. He has reported vulnerabilities through bug bounty programs, with advisories published by ZDI, alongside active participation on the HackerOne and YesWeHack platforms. His work covers Windows application analysis, applied research, and vulnerability discovery in corporate environments and widely used products.

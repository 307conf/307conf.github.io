Daniele Nazaré Tavares holds a master’s degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering from ITA and a bachelor’s degree in Computer Engineering from INATEL, working on projects that combine software, hardware, data modeling, and Artificial Intelligence.

She has helped organize mid- and large-scale events such as Flisol Santa Rita do Sapucaí (2018 and 2019) and multiple editions of The Developer Conference (TDC), where she also served as a volunteer coordinator. Daniele regularly shares knowledge at events like CNPPD, TDC, Python Brasil, Campus Party, H2HC, BSides, and Latinoware, covering topics including data science, cybersecurity, AI, quantum computing, and the impact of emerging technologies.

Thiago José Lucas is a Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence Researcher at UNESP in Bauru, with extensive academic training and experience in teaching and research. He is a postdoctoral researcher at the Advanced Laboratory in Computer Networks Security (LARS/UNESP), where he also completed his doctorate and master’s degrees in Computer Science.

Thiago serves as a University Professor at Saint Leo University (Florida, USA) and as a Professor of Higher Education at the Faculty of Technology of Ourinhos. He is also a full-time Researcher at Centro Paula Souza. His career covers intrusion detection, machine learning applied to security, and defense systems based on ensemble learning.

Davi Trindade is the cofounder of the hacking community hackersOnSteroids and works as a pentester and red teamer focused on mobile, web, Active Directory, and applied research in malware development.

He has presented research and studies at events such as H2HC 2024, BSides SP 2025, HNWD SP 2025, and in multiple security communities. Davi aims to share knowledge and support new professionals entering the offensive security ecosystem.

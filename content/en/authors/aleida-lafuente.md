Aleida Lafuente has been working in Cybersecurity for over three years, developing a security-first mindset focused on protecting payment systems within PCI DSS and ISO 27001 controlled environments.

Throughout her experience, she has proposed IAM initiatives, participated in incident response activities, conducted proof-of-concept evaluations for Threat Intelligence tools, and engaged in security awareness programs. She has hands-on experience with tools such as Cortex XDR and IBM QRadar. Currently, she focuses on reconnaissance practices, writing Bug Bounty reports, and preparing for the Security+ certification.

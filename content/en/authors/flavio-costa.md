Flávio Costa has 18 years of experience working with companies such as Cisco, Dell, and Fortinet. Throughout his career, he has contributed to the **MITRE ATT&CK** framework, created the **C3P-Model**, and earned more than 50 industry certifications.

He currently leads strategic initiatives that have generated over USD 88 million in pipeline and has developed global solutions to combat ransomware. Flávio is also an author, speaker, and CompTIA Subject Matter Expert (SME), contributing to the definition of industry standards and best practices.

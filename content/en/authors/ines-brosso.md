Inês Brosso is a university professor and researcher. She holds a degree in Mathematics from PUC-SP and a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the Polytechnic School at USP. She has more than 30 years of experience at large companies such as Asea Brown Boveri and Banco Bradesco S.A., working on secure software development and information security.

She is the author of several international papers and the book *Quantum Computing: A New Era*. Her research focuses on security, cryptography, and quantum computing.

Erikson Júlio de Aguiar is a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of São Paulo (USP), with a research internship at the University of Florida. His research focuses on security and robustness of AI and Machine Learning systems, particularly for medical applications.

He received the **Best Student Award** at IEEE CBMS 2024 and works toward the development of ethical, robust, and trustworthy AI systems for healthcare. Erikson also holds a master’s degree from USP, where he researched blockchain solutions for medical data privacy.

Fernando Mengali is a cybersecurity researcher focused on pentesting and AppSec, with more than 15 years of experience in offensive security. A specialist in 0-day discovery and exploit development, he has published over 135 CVEs and contributed to repositories such as Packetstorm, CXSecurity, and Exploit-DB.

He created yrprey.com — a framework with more than 20 OWASP-vulnerable projects for offensive security practice — and also developed leapfix.co, a static code analysis plugin with thousands of downloads, as well as the fitoxs.com platform for AI-driven dynamic application analysis.

His work spans advanced research, reverse engineering, and practical demonstrations of vulnerability exploitation in critical environments.

Anderson Nascimento is the director and principal security researcher at Allele Security Intelligence. He has more than 10 years of experience providing high-level research for companies and international clients.

As a researcher, he has developed and published proof-of-concept exploits for Linux and FreeBSD kernel vulnerabilities, backed by solid knowledge of the x86 architecture, operating systems, and — above all — Linux kernel attack and defense.

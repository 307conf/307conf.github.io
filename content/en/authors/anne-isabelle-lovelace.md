Anne Isabelle “Anya” Lovelace is a trans and autistic woman who has worked at several big companies — even if, as she puts it, none of them really matter. She is the author of “Hacking Inacabado de Verão” in Tramoia.sh 0x01, exploring the security of Google devices and celebrating hacking driven by curiosity and fun.

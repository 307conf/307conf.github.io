Together, Willian Caprino and Carlos Cabral present a complete update on the production process of the documentary dedicated to the Brazilian hacker scene. The talk shares details on the project’s progress, lessons learned, challenges, and behind-the-scenes research.

During the session, the authors also screen an exclusive 17-minute teaser, revealing part of the material already produced and offering a preview of the historical deep dive the documentary aims to deliver on Brazil’s hacker culture.

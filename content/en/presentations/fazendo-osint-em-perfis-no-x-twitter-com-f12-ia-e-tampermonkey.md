The talk presents practical OSINT techniques applied to X/Twitter profiles, using accessible tools like the browser's DevTools (F12), artificial intelligence, and scripts via Tampermonkey.

The session shows how to extract relevant information, automate collection, and enrich digital investigations from social media.

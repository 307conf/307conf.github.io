The evolution of quantum computers expands the ability to solve complex problems but also imposes significant challenges on information security. Widely used cryptographic techniques are being questioned as quantum algorithms advance.

In this talk, Inês Brosso covers the fundamentals of quantum cryptography and discusses how this new era impacts cybersecurity. The session offers a practical reflection on how to approach this emerging technology and how professionals and organizations should prepare for a post-quantum landscape.

This talk dives into the hidden and offensive side of Artificial Intelligence, exploring how algorithms and automation are being leveraged to execute increasingly sophisticated cyberattacks. The session covers threat vectors ranging from automated social engineering to the exploitation of weaknesses in AI systems and models.

Throughout the presentation, the impact of these techniques on the modern security landscape is discussed, highlighting how automation and algorithm-driven decision-making are reshaping the digital battlefield. The goal is to provide a critical and realistic view of the risks and challenges organizations face in an AI-driven world.

In this hands-on workshop, participants will explore **Gandalf**, one of the first open *prompt injection* challenges available on the internet. The activity proposes a hands-on approach to understanding how language models can be manipulated through carefully crafted inputs.

The workshop is focused on experimentation and practical learning, helping participants understand real risks, the limitations of AI models, and fundamental concepts of security applied to LLMs.
